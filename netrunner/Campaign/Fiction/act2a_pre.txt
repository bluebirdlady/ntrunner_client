RUSH JOB
by Calvin Wong Tze Loon 黃子倫

The room rumbled with the noisome grousing of Loonies who hadn't had enough caffeine put in them to deal with this crap, but they quieted up when Shreya cleared her throat.

"Monday morning, Blues," she said. "Hope you all had a great weekend."

The crew hooted. Shreya smiled, watching the tension in the room dissolve.

"I need you all out on H-nine eight two again. This is a storage job. Blue Sun's worried about capacity overflow on their Hee-three tanks, so they're looking to expand some of the current facilities. They've asked for the best crew we have, so I'm sending you."

"Bluesway!" shouted someone near the back, and the whole room echoed the cry.

"And I've saved the best part for last." Shreya turned her PAD around so her crew could see the shiny violet work bill: RUSH ORDER. The room erupted in cheers.

---

On the maglev, Tereshchenko slid up beside her. "You said you negotiated extra on this project. Do you think you can ask Corporate if I can get that in advance? My kid's tuition just gets higher and higher…"

"Don't sweat it, Hanna," Shreya said, swiping her PAD toward her. "I'll advance you."

"Shreya, that's—"

"I'll take it out of your next job. Don't worry about it."

"One day," Tereshchenko smiled, "one of those higher-ups is gonna ask where all that discretionary you always have comes from."

"Know any good lawyers?" Shreya shot back.

Returning her attention to her PAD, Shreya shuffled a few lines around in the ledgers. Before the train pulled into the platform, her little transaction had disappeared without trace. Just another rounding error in the creaking mass of Weyland accounting.

---

They don't know what's really stored in those tanks.

The data you pulled from Kaguya points here: a Weyland Consortium subsidiary facility, Luna surface level. The manifest says construction materials. The encrypted partition says something else entirely.

Shreya and her Blues trust that the work is legitimate. They do their jobs. They don't ask questions.

The corp is counting on that.

TARGET: Weyland Consortium: Built to Last
FACILITY: Blue Sun Helium-3 Processing, Heinlein Surface
OBJECTIVE: Access the encrypted partition. Find out what they're hiding up there.
