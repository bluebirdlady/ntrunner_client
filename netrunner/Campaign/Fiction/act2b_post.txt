WHAT YOU FOUND

The data is fragmentary. Encrypted in layers you've never seen before.

What you can read is enough.

Jinteki Research Division — Project Designation: PALE HORSE — is not a construction project. It is not helium extraction. It is not any of the mundane operations the facility manifest claims.

The clones working the surface facilities don't know what they're building. They were told it was infrastructure. Most of them believe it. A few have started to ask questions.

Those who ask questions stop showing up for shifts.

The runner underground is already circulating fragments. Someone with access to Jinteki's internal comms intercepted a message three days ago: a directive to "accelerate the timeline." No one knows what the timeline is for.

You do now.

The question isn't whether to act.
The question is: who do you trust with this?

The corps have their informants everywhere. The newsies will bury it or sensationalise it into noise. The runner networks are fractious, paranoid, and brilliant.

You got in. You got out. You have the data.

The next run is the one that matters.
