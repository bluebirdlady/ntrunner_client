BREACH DETECTED
by Laury "CaKnuckleguy" Plant

Harvey sat up. It was his thirteenth night shift in a row and he was really sick of this overly sensitive firewall going off every couple of hours. He took this data-babysitting job because it was easy money and he could watch the latest Miranda Rhapsody holo-novel in peace. He had a comfy chair, holo-sculpts of his kids on his desk. No one bothered him.

SYSTEM BREACH ATTEMPT DETECTED.

"Damn it, come on, you piece of crap. If you're going to get triggered by every brush-by or sniffer program…"

He punched a quick command and the glaring red light shut off. Grumbling, Harvey made a mental note to suppress that alarm in the future. No one was going to break into KOVIN & MARKS DIGITAL VAULT SERVICES. They had nothing of value. Old data that companies kept around for regulatory purposes, didn't want to clutter up their own servers with. Digital paperwork, old plans that never saw fruition. Nothing current.

SYSTEM BREACH DETECTED.

"What do you want, dammit?!"

He reread the red letters hanging in space, noticing a missing word. They were no longer warning of an attempt.

"Breach… Detected? That can't be right."

SERVER 121120 ACCESSED. UNKNOWN POINT OF ORIGIN.

TRACE SUBROUTINE FAILED.
ORIGINS UNKNOWN.
ATTEMPTING SERVER LOCKDOWN.

"Oh no. No no no."

Harvey's hand slammed down on the big red holo-button: EMERGENCY ICE ACTIVATION. Time to call in the cavalry.

ICE ACTIVATION SEQUENCE BYPASSED.
UNABLE TO COMPLY.

"What? That's impossible!"

DOWNLOAD SEQUENCE COMPLETED.
SYSTEM REBOOT INITIATED.
TERMINATING PROCESS TREE.
SYSTEM SHUTDOWN COMPLETE.

His monitors went blank. All of them.

He sank back into his chair. This was not how the night was supposed to go.

Somewhere in the corporate tower above, an alert was already propagating up the chain. A flag had been raised in a system no one was supposed to know existed. And whoever had placed that flag knew exactly what they were looking for.

The question was what they would do now that they'd found it.

The corps are already asking questions.
Make the next run count.
