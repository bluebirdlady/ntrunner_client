GACHAPON
by Morgan "Anzekay" White

It was raining.

It always seemed to be raining here, like nature itself was committed to ensuring your boots were always dripping wet by the time you reached the busted old escalator. Relic neon signs and shattered glass lay strewn across the floor of the dilapidated mall, mixed in with trash and scraps of rusted metal.

Lee skirted around the bulk of the puddles and refuse, before taking the second right down what used to be the tech alley. A brief roar of thunder from overhead, and shutters across the windows rattled with deafening intensity. At least the weather meant they were unlikely to bump into any other runners.

At the final shopfront of the alley, before it ended at a rusted chain link fence, Lee stopped. A flick of their wrist brought their AR overlay winking into view. A few sweeps of the PICM scanner showed the all clear. Lee gingerly stepped up to the still-intact door. First time they'd come here and found the place clear of anti-personnel devices.

"RO-O ARC-DE", read the loose and decayed sign above the arcade's door, its neon tubes long since broken.

Walking inside was like travelling centuries back in time. Rows of dilapidated pinball machines, arcade cabinets with games now lost to time. The centre of the cramped room was dominated by several large pod-like machines: early attempts to create VR-like gaming experiences.

The growing storm rattled the metal blinds again.

Then the screen of a nearby machine flickered to life.

TEXT SCROLLED ACROSS IT: YOU HAVE SOMETHING WE WANT TO KNOW ABOUT.

Lee kept very still.

WE HAVE SOMETHING YOU NEED.

WE KNOW ABOUT THE WEYLAND RUN. WE KNOW WHAT YOU FOUND.

THERE'S A JINTEKI RESEARCH DIVISION ON THIS SAME NETWORK.
WHAT THEY'RE DOING IS WORSE.
WE CAN GIVE YOU A WAY IN.

The machine whirred. A small plastic capsule dropped into the tray below.

Lee reached down and picked it up. Inside: a memchip, and a single printed card with a server address.

THE PRICE IS INFORMATION. THEIRS, AND YOURS.

YOUR MOVE.

—

TARGET: Jinteki: Replicating Perfection
FACILITY: Research Division — Heinlein Network Node
OBJECTIVE: Access the research archive. Find out what they're growing up there.
